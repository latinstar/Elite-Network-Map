Aghajari Airport ([[IATA airport code|IATA]]: AKW[3], [[ICAO airport code|ICAO]]: OIAG) is an [[Airport|airport]] serving [[Omidiyeh]],[1] a city in and the capital of [[Central District]], in [[Omidiyeh County]], [[Khuzestan Province]], [[Iran]].
The airport resides at an [[Elevation|elevation]] of 88 feet (27 m) above [[Mean sea level|mean sea level]].
It has one [[Runway|runway]] designated 13/31 with an [[Asphalt concrete|asphalt]] surface measuring 2,113 by 45 metres (6,932 ft × 148 ft).[1]
