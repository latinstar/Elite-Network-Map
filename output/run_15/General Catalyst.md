[[General Catalyst]] exerts strategic influence through significant investments in defense technology companies such as [[Anduril Industries]] and [[Helsing]]. Demonstrating its organizational power within the venture capital community, [[General Catalyst]] mobilized over 110 firms to sign a joint statement of support for [[Silicon Valley Bank]] during its collapse. [[General Catalyst]] plans to acquire and convert [[Summa Health]], a non-profit health system, into a for-profit entity, aiming to use it as a testing ground for advanced technologies, including [[generative artificial intelligence]] solutions from its portfolio companies, thereby influencing healthcare innovation and delivery. In a move to shape public health technology, [[General Catalyst]] launched a £30 million medical technology venture fund in collaboration with the [[UK National Health Service]] and [[Speedinvest]]. Through strategic acquisitions, [[General Catalyst]] expanded its network of influence by integrating German venture capital firm [[La Famiglia]], with its founding partner [[Jeannette zu Fürstenberg]] becoming a managing director for GC's European business, and acquiring Indian early-stage investor [[Venture Highway]].
