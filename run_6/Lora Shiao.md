Lora Shiao is an American government official who had served as the chief operating officer[1] for the [[Director of National Intelligence|Office of the Director of National Intelligence]].
Shiao served as an intelligence briefer to the United States attorney general and [[Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation]] from 2005 to 2007.[5]
She was appointed to the senior national intelligence service within the [[National Counterterrorism Center]] (NCTC) in May 2014.
Shiao was named Chief Operating Officer for the [[Office of the Director of National Intelligence]] (ODNI) in October 2020.
